Mother Teresa

“If you can't feed a hundred people, feed just one.”


Birthdate: August 26, 1910
Place of Birth: Skopje, North Macedonia

  1. Mother Teresa, a Roman Catholic nun, founded the Missionaries of Charity.
  2. She dedicated her life to serving the destitute and dying.
  3. Mother Teresa established hospices and centers worldwide.
  4. In 1979, she was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize for her selfless work.
  5. Her simple lifestyle and profound impact led to beatification in 2003.
  6. Mother Teresa was canonized as a saint in 2016.
  7. Her legacy continues through the ongoing efforts of the Missionaries of Charity. They provide care and compassion to the needy globally.
